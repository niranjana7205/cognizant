Cognizant

Cognizant Technology Solutions (CTS) isa multinational IT services company that provides IT consulting, business process outsourcing (BPO), and digital solutions. Founded in 1993, the company is headquartered in Teaneck, New Jersey, with a significant presence in India. Cognizant helps businesses modernize technology, reimagine processes, and transform experiences. The company is known for its focus on digital transformation and leveraging emerging technologies like AI and cloud computing.
